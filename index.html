The real problem in the field

  • Machines fail under time pressure, and diagnosis must start immediately
  • Relevant repair knowledge exists, but is scattered across systems and notes
  • Engineers rely on experience and intuition — not documentation
  • Finding the right reference often takes longer than identifying the issue itself

The bottleneck is not expertise. It is time-to-signal during fault isolation.

What changes with this system

  • Relevant machine context is accessible during diagnosis
  • Previous failures and fixes are searchable in seconds
  • QR codes remove navigation overhead in the field
  • Engineers spend less time searching, more time fixing
  • Knowledge becomes a support layer, not a dependency

Designed for real engineering environments

  • Does not replace engineering expertise
  • Reduces time spent finding relevant information
  • Works alongside existing maintenance processes
  • Can be self-hosted inside industrial infrastructure
