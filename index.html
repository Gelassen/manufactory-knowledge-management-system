The Problem

  • Knowledge stored in notebooks
  • Repair information spread across messengers and spreadsheets
  • Experience remembered by individual engineers
  • Previous repairs difficult to search later

As a result, previous maintenance experience becomes hard to reuse.

The Solution

  • Machine registry
  • Machine breakdown history
  • Documented troubleshooting procedures
  • Searchable maintenance records
  • Self-hosted deployment

Open Source

  • MIT License
  • Self-hosted
  • No vendor lock-in
  • Customizable source code
  • Your data remains inside your infrastructure
